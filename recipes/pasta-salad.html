Pasta Salad

Description

Description goes here

Ingredients

  • 1 package (16 oz) fusilli pasta
  • 3 cups halved cherry tomatoes
  • ½ pound cubed provolone cheese
  • ½ pound cubed salami
  • ¼ pound pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 can (10oz) black olives, drained
  • 1 jar (4oz) pimentos, drained
  • 1 bottle (8oz) Italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta, cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente.
  3. Drain then rinse pasta with cold water.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos.
  5. Toss with salad dressing.
