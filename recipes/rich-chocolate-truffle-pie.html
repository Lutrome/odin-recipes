Rich Chocolate Truffle Pie

Description

A no-bake chocolate pie in a chocolate cookie crust, with whipped cream on top.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • ¼ cup sifted confectioner's sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 9-inch prepared chocolate cookie crumb crust

Steps

  1. Combine chocolate chips and ½ of the heavy whipping cream in a bowl, and microwave for 1-2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds.
  2. Cool the mixture to room temperature, then stir in sugar and vanilla. Set aside.
  3. In another small bowl, beat the rest of the heavy cream until soft peaks form.
  4. In a mixer, beat in the chocolate mixture at high speed, &frac13 at a time, with the cream.
  5. Mix well and spoon into the crust. Refrigerate for 8 hours minimum before serving.
