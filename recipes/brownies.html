Ingredients

Brownie Batter

  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • &frac13 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp baking powder

Frosting

  • 3 tbsp softened butter
  • 3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confectioner's sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Grease and flour an 8-inch pan.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt ½ cup butter.
  4. Remove saucepan from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 tsp vanilla.
  5. Beat in &frac13 cup cocoa powder, ½ cup flour, salt, and baking powder.
  6. Spread batter into pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, being careful not to overcook.
  8. Remove brownies and set aside.
  9. Combine 3 tbsp softened butter, 3 tbsp cocoa powder, honey, 1 tbsp vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioner's sugar.
  10. Stir until smooth, and frost brownies while they're still warm.
